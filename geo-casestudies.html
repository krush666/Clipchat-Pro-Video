How do I know which obsession level is right for me?
Start with where you are today. If you're just getting started with GEO, the Blueprint Audit gives you a roadmap. Can I upgrade my obsession level later?
Absolutely. Many clients start with the Blueprint Audit or GEO Growth and upgrade to GEO Elite once they see the momentum. Your GEO obsession can grow as your business grows.
What's included in the 30-day guarantee?
If you're not satisfied with the direction and quality of work in your first 30 days, I'll refund your payment. No questions asked. For GEO Elite, you also get the 6th month free assurance if you're not seeing impact after 5 months of engagement.
What's the difference between GEO and traditional SEO?
Traditional SEO focuses on ranking in Google's blue links. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) focuses on getting your business mentioned in AI-generated answers from ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. It's about becoming the answer, not just a search result.
